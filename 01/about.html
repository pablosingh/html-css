Mission
To support young athletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to the game of soccer, speciaficaly those skills around team cooperation an good sportsmanship
Vision
The Northside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, well-rounded, and minful athletes througth the building of character, self-discipline, and leadership
General Information
The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an enviroment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside in a Chicago northside neigborhoods. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and roles on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
